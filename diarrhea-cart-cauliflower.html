About the product

Cauliflower is made up of tightly bound clusters of soft, crumbly, sweet cauliflower florets that form a dense head. Read more about the Cauliflower here.

Storage and Uses

  • Refrigerate in a loosely sealed plastic bag. It keeps well for upto 7 days. Do not store florets for more than 5 days.
  • Cooked and used in soups, rice, curries, gobi parathas( flat breads), one of the most liked recipes.
