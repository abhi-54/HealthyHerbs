About the product

Healthy Herbs brings to you trimmed crowns of Broccoli that are ready to be chopped for your favorite dish. Just give them a quick rinse first and then they're ready to be steamed or boiled. Read more about the Broccoli here.

Storage and Uses

  • Consume fresh broccoli as soon as you can as it will not keep for long. To store, drizzle water droplets on the flower heads, cover loosely in damp paper towels and refrigerate.
  • Never wash broccoli before storing in the refrigerator. The excess moisture promotes mold.
  • Do not store broccoli in a sealed container or plastic bag. Raw broccolis requires air circulation.
  • Brocollis serve as a healthy raw vegetable. Add them to your salads, pasta, curries and rice. Steam broccoli stalks, add some salt and oil and roast it for a crunchy flavour.
