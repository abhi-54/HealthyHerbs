About the product

With high water content and crunchy flesh, Cucumbers have striped, light to dark green coloured skin that is edible. Read more about Cucumber here.

Storage and Uses

  • Wrap the cucumbers tightly in a plastic wrap and store them in the refrigerator. It minimizes decay and keeps it fresh for a long time.
  • Cucumbers can be eaten fresh. They are a healthy snack to munch on when you are bored. Used in salads, soups, sandwiches, rolls, curries, cocktails and pickles.
