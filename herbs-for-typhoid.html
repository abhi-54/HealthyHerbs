The best Typhoid-fighting foods

Eating foods don't protect people against typhoid completely. The term typhoid-fighting foods refers to foods that may lower the risk of developing typhoid if a person adds them to their diet.

Foods that contain naturally occurring compounds that have potent anti-typhoid properties include:

Banana

Banana, especially when supplemented by skimmed milk or buttermilk, makes a desirable food for the maintenance of nutrition in typhoid patients.

It supplies caloric requirements, minerals, and vitamins, and is apparently readily digestible.

Apricot

Apricot is water content-rich fruit. It helps in restoring the water content in the body that gets depleted during typhoid fever and cause dehydration.

Watermelon

Watermelons have excellent hydrating properties with 90% water content.

It is also a great source for A, C, B-complex vitamins, iron and fiber which boosts body metabolism.

Grapes

Grapes are water content-rich fruit with 90% water. It helps in restoring the water content in the body that gets depleted during typhoid fever and cause dehydration.

Grapes offer a wealth of health benefits due to their high nutrient and antioxidant contents

Potato

Potatoes are high in content of carbohydrates as well as high in calories.

Therefore, potato is also recommended for typhoid patients.

Cucumber

Cucumbers also have an excellent hydrating properties with rich water content.

Cucumber juice is also recommended for typhoid patients.
