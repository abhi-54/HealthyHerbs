Storage and Uses

  • Refrigerate in a loosely sealed plastic bag. It keeps well for upto 4 days.
  • Spinach can be eaten raw or cooked. Cooked spinach help in absorption of essential minerals in the body.
  • It can be boiled and added to salads, pastas, soups or fried and used in dal and curries.
