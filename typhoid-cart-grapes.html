Grapes, Green

About the product

Juicy and snappy, with a beautiful balance of sweet and tart flavour, these grape bunches have a lingering taste.

Storage and Uses

  • Wash the grapes under cool, running water. Pat them dry and store it in the refrigerator.
  • Rinse well and eat them right off the vine for a healthy, refreshing snack any time of day. You can also freeze them and toss them in drinks.
  • Make your salads and curd rice colourful by adding these refreshing and sweet grapes.
