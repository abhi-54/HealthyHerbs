About the product

The juicy, sweet and grainy textured flesh is filled with 12-14% of sugar content, making it a healthy alternative to sugary carbonated drinks. Read more about the Watermelon here.

Storage and Uses

  • Store them in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight.
  • Cut and serve the melons chilled.
  • Can be eaten fresh- scoop them up with ice creams or in fruit salads.Spice up your watermelon with a tinge of mint leaves.
  • Rind can be used to prepare pickles, curries and pies. Seeds can be roasted with salt and consumed.
