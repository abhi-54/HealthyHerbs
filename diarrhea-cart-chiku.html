Chiku

About the product

Brown skinned chikus are smooth to grainy textured, musky-scented and deliciously sweet in taste. The flesh generally contains 2-3 large and inedible black seeds.

Storage and Uses

  • Store them in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight.
  • Chikus can be eaten fresh or savoured with salads and custards.
  • As they are soft and easily consumable, they can be offered to growing babies.
