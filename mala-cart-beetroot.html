Beetroot, Grade-A

About the product

These edible ruby red roots are smooth and bulbous and have the highest sugar content than any other vegetable.

Storage and Uses

  • While storing beets, trim the leaves 2 inches from the root soon after you buy them as they sap the moisture from the beet root.
  • The root bulbs should also be put into a bag and can be stored in the refrigerator for a maximum of 7 to 10 days.
